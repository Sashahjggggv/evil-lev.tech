PERFECT LIFE CONSTRUCTION PROGRESS – NOVEMBER 2023

November 2023
27
to share

In November 2023:

  • In the I section:
    • Mounted elevators;
    • Lined ceramic tiles in the corridors of floors 7-6;
    • Facade work continued.

 

  • In the II section:
    • The plastering of the walls of floors 2-7 has been completed;
    • The semi-dry screed of 6-7 floors has been completed;
    • Facade work continued;
    • An elevator has been installed;
    • Laying of external networks (sewerage) continued.

 

  • In the III section:
    • Heating and water supply is 100% completed;
    • Electric cables to apartments are 100% laid;
    • The laying of low-current networks has been completed by 100%;
    • Plastering of the walls of floors 3-6 was completed;
    • Facade work continued.

 

  • In the IV section:
    • Windows installed at 100%;
    • Partition walls are completely bricked;
    • Heating and water supply of apartments is 100% completed;
    • The laying of low-current networks has been completed in full;
    • Electric cables are laid to the apartments on floors 2-5.

 

  • In the V section:
    • Masonry of the facade walls is 99% completed;
    • Masonry of internal walls and partitions is 60% completed;
    • Insulation of the roof with side screeds was made, gluing of 1 layer of roofing material was started;
    • Installation of window structures has begun.

 

  • In the VI section:
    • The foundation slab is concreted.

 

  • In Section IX:
    • We are reinforcing the floor slab of the 7th floor;
    • We are building the outer walls of the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd floors.

 

  • Parking between V and IX sections:
    • Monolithic works are 75% completed.
