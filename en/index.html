WHY WE LEV DEVELOPMENT?

To change the world, you need to apply the global experience locally. That is why in 2015 Oleksandr Ostrovsky together with Vasyl Levitsky founded LEV Development. 

The title has a double meaning. LEV Development is about Lviv. And LEV is: Life , Energy, and Victory. We believe that life and energy lead us to victory.

LEV Development – for change and moving forward
People want to live in beautiful and safe houses, walk on comfortable roads and sidewalks, go to cool shops and stay in hotels with excellent service. And our goal is to create this comfort for you by thinking through projects down to the smallest detail: from the concept to the last nail. Urban planners and architects working with LEV put a separate idea into each project, which is read as soon as you enter the new building. We are “burning” to change the world by adapting it to the needs of the people, not the other way around.

LEV Development – for a smart investment
For those for whom real estate is, above all, a successful investment, we have created a chain hotel near the Bukovel shopping mall and a new apart-hotel LEV City Hotel. Becoming the owner of an apartment in one of them, you get a good object for investment.

So, let’s change the world together?
