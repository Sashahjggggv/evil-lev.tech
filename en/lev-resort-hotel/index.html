HOLIDAY AMAZING

LEV Resort Hotel is the only hotel located near Bukovel Shopping Center with the possibility of privately owned apartments being purchased. Thanks to the excellent location - 700 meters from the lift 1R of the parking lot # 1 - it is planned high occupancy of rooms - up to 65% per year. The full term of payback of the real estate is planned within 6-10 years, the first profit will arrive at the accounts of the owners in the first months of the hotel. The hotel offers one-, two- and three-bedroom apartments as well as Standard rooms. The Standard rooms, apart from the living room, have a bathroom, shower and loggia, and the Premier, Suite and Presidential rooms have a kitchen area, bath and terrace. The hotel complex includes a spa, fitness room, children's games room, restaurant, bar and underground parking. Each room will be equipped with appliances, furniture, plumbing and decorative accessories.

REASONSTO CHOOSE LEV RESORT HOTEL

Management company

01.

Ribas Hotels Group is a national hotel operator that will manage LEV Resort Hotel. Ribas Hotels Group manages and develops city hotels, resort hotels and apartment hotels. Ensures stable occupancy of hotels and returns on investors' investments by developing investment projects. Ribas Hotels Group will be responsible for complex management of apartments and providing first-class service to guests.

Panoramic view

02.

The hotel stands on a hillside above the Prutets River, so every window on any floor offers a beautiful view of the surrounding landscape. All rooms have terraces that allow you to observe nature without leaving the room. The exterior of the building is designed in such a way that hotel guests can admire the ski slopes. The panoramic windows of the terraces create the effect of increasing the space in the rooms.

Security system

03.

An innovative security system will allow families with children to stay and not worry about their health. The entrance to the hotel territory is planned to be equipped with a checkpoint. Video surveillance will be organized on all floors. The elevators will be equipped with an access control system. All terraces are equipped with reliable protective structures. On the territory of the hotel will be organized a permanent patrol of the security service. LEV Resort Hotel can rightfully be called a safety zone.

The best restaurants

04.

Hotel visitors are offered the services of an excellent European restaurant and cafe. A varied menu, first-class service and a cozy atmosphere are all you need for a wonderful vacation. From the restaurant, you can go to an open terrace with a wonderful view of the slope of Mount Bukovel. Exquisite dishes can be ordered directly to the room, if you wish to enjoy their taste in the circle of loved ones.

Children room

05.

Hotel LEV Resort is a place to relax with children. The construction of a children's playground is planned here, which will allow children to play happily, and parents to free up some time for themselves. In the play area, your child will be under the constant supervision of experienced educators. Interesting classes and educational programs will not let her get bored. A play area with a slide, a sandbox and a carousel will also be organized. An excellent rest for your children is guaranteed.
